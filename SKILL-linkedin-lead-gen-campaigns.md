## Core Strategy: Transformation-First Outreach

**NEVER sell "ChatGPT seats." ALWAYS sell solved problems.**

The messaging framework follows these rules:

1. **Lead with the pain** — reference a real problem in their industry
2. **Name the outcome** — what changes when AI is deployed correctly
3. **Position as partner** — not vendor, not SaaS rep, not reseller
4. **Create urgency** — competitors are already adopting AI
5. **Keep it human** — no corporate jargon, write like a smart colleague

---

## Campaign Architecture: 3-Step Sequence

Every campaign follows this proven sequence:

### Step 1: Connection Request (Day 0)

- **300 character limit** — must be concise and specific
- Reference their industry, role, or company
- Ask to connect, don't pitch
- Goal: Get accepted

### Step 2: Value-First Follow-Up (Day 2-3 after acceptance)

- **8,000 character limit** — use more space
- Lead with a relevant insight or stat about their industry
- Mention a specific use case for their role
- Soft CTA — offer a free audit, resource, or quick chat
- Link to ElevaIQ.com or a specific industry page

### Step 3: Final Nudge (Day 5-7 after Step 2)

- Shorter, casual, reference the previous message
- Reframe the value prop from a different angle
- Direct CTA — "Want me to send over the starter pack?"
- Include a link to schedule or to the site

## Meet Alfred Campaign Creation Steps (Step-by-Step)

1. Go to https://app.meetalfred.com
2. Click **"+ Create Campaign"** (top right)
3. Select **"LinkedIn Campaign"**
4. Source: **"LinkedIn Search"**
5. Paste the LinkedIn Search URL for the target industry
6. Click **Continue**
7. Choose **"Build sequence manually"**
8. Add Step 1: **"Send Connection Request"** → paste connection message
9. Enable **"Auto follow-up with message once connected"**
10. Paste follow-up message → set delay to 3 days
11. Add Step 2: **"Send Message"** → paste nudge message → set delay to 5 days
12. Click **Continue** → **Review and Publish**
13. Name campaign: `[Industry] - [Role] - [YYYY-MM-DD]`
14. **Publish** — campaign runs during work hours
