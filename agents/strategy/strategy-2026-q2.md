## The 3 Engines (in priority order)

### Engine 1 — Compliance Lead Magnet (HIGHEST LEVERAGE, BUILD NOW)

**What:** AI Compliance Assessment (see SPEC-ai-compliance-assessment.md)
**Why this first:** Converts the traffic we're already generating. Without it, the blog is a publicity stunt. With it, every compliance-anxious visitor becomes a captured lead with a stated risk tier.
**Timeline:** 2-3 day build, this week
**Owner:** Chris + Cursor/Claude Code
**Compounds for:** 12+ months (compliance fear doesn't go away; if anything it escalates as more states pass laws)

### Engine 2 — Partner Channel Multipliers (BUILD WEEKS 2-4)

**What:** Pre-aggregator partnerships where one relationship = 50-200 SMB referrals
**Channels (priority order):**

1. **Vertical MSPs / IT consultants** — they sell ChatGPT Business to their existing book; we revenue-share. Target: 5 active partnerships by July 1.
2. **State CPA associations + bar associations** — CLE-eligible compliance webinars. Target: 3 webinars run by July 1.
3. **Vertical SaaS co-marketing** — Clio (legal), Athenahealth (med), JobNimbus (trades), Gusto (HR). They have the SMBs, we have the OpenAI badge.
4. **HR/PEO networks** — every PEO customer needs an AI policy now; this is the compliance wedge applied at scale.

**Why not direct outbound:** a $25/seat product can't support outbound CAC. Channels stack the economics.

### Engine 3 — AEO Citation Farm (ALREADY RUNNING, INSTRUMENT BETTER)

**What:** 30+ blog posts + daily auto-publish loop
**The shift:** stop optimizing for SEO rank, start optimizing for AI citation share. Perplexity, ChatGPT search, AI Overview, Claude — these are increasingly the discovery layer.
**Tactics:**

- Direct answers in first 50 words of every H2
- FAQPage schema on every post
- Named-entity dense (companies, laws, dates, $ figures)
- Comparison tables (citation magnets)
- Track citation share weekly across 4 engines
