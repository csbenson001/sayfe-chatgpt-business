The Strategy in 60 Seconds

Channel priority: Google Search Ads first (capture intent), LinkedIn second (vertical targeting), retargeting on Meta third (cheap reinforcement). Skip broad social awareness spend entirely — the unit economics don't justify it for a $30/seat referral product.

The math that makes this work: One converted customer with 10 seats generates $150/month in commission for Year 1, then $60/month for Year 2. That's $2,520 lifetime commission per customer. If you acquire that customer for $50-150 in ad spend, you're looking at a 15-45x return. Even a single conversion per month at $100 CAC makes this campaign profitable from day one.

Why Google Search wins: People actively searching "chatgpt for teams" or "chatgpt business pricing" have purchase intent. The average Google Search CPC is $2-6 for these keywords — roughly 10x cheaper per click than ChatGPT's own $60 CPM ads. And these searchers are already educated on the product; they just need a trusted partner to guide them through.

Critical dependency: This entire strategy activates the moment you get OpenAI partner approval. The approval badge ("Official OpenAI Partner") is your #1 conversion asset — it's the trust signal that separates you from generic AI consulting sites. Without it, the landing pages still work, but conversion rates will be significantly lower.

Break-Even Analysis

At $2,500/month spend: You need just 2 customers with ~8 seats each to break even on ad spend in Month 1 (from recurring commission alone). That's extremely achievable.

CAC target: Anything under $500/customer is excellent (LTV:CAC > 5x). Anything under $250/customer is outstanding (LTV:CAC > 10x).

Scale trigger: Once you hit 5+ customers/month consistently with CAC under $300, double your Google Ads budget immediately. The marginal economics only get better as Google's algorithm optimizes with more conversion data.
