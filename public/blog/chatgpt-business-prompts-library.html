How to Use These Prompts

Using these prompts is simple:

  1. Copy: Click the "Copy Prompt" button on any prompt box
  2. Paste: Open ChatGPT and paste the prompt into the chat
  3. Customize: Replace bracketed placeholders [LIKE_THIS] with your specific details

Pro Tips

Be specific: The more detail you provide in the brackets, the better ChatGPT's response. "Finance team" is good, but "Finance team of 5 managing $2M annual budget" is better.

Iterate: ChatGPT often improves with follow-up questions. Ask for adjustments, more examples, or different tones.

Chain prompts: Use output from one prompt as input for another. For example, use the "Competitive Analysis" output to inform "Marketing Campaign Brief."

Create custom GPTs: Build team-specific GPTs using these prompts as instructions in the "System" section of your custom GPT configuration.
