When OpenAI launched its SMB Channel Partner Program, it created an official path for businesses to get ChatGPT Business with expert deployment support — at the exact same price as buying direct. The catch? Not everyone calling themselves an "OpenAI partner" is in the authorized program. And even among legitimate partners, the quality of onboarding, industry expertise, and ongoing support varies significantly. This guide tells you exactly what to look for.

What Is an Authorized OpenAI SMB Channel Partner?

An authorized OpenAI SMB Channel Partner is a company that OpenAI has vetted and approved to provision ChatGPT Business subscriptions and provide partner-certified support. These partners:

  • Provision the official ChatGPT Business product directly through OpenAI infrastructure
  • Charge the same price as OpenAI direct — no markup is allowed
  • Do not have access to customer ChatGPT conversations or business data
  • Are compensated by OpenAI through a revenue-share on subscriptions they bring in

This is different from affiliates, referral programs, or resellers, which may not be authorized to provision the product directly and may not be subject to OpenAI's partner quality standards.

Watch out: "OpenAI partner" is sometimes used loosely. Anyone can become an OpenAI API customer and call themselves an "OpenAI partner." The SMB Channel Partner Program is a specific, invitation-based program. Always confirm partner status and that pricing matches OpenAI direct before signing anything.

5 Criteria for Choosing the Best ChatGPT Business Partner

✓ 1. Verified Authorization

Confirm the partner is in the official OpenAI SMB Channel Partner Program — not a referral program or affiliate. The product should be provisioned directly through OpenAI.

✓ 2. No Pricing Markup

ChatGPT Business must be $25/user/month (monthly) or $20/user/month (annual) — identical to openai.com. Any premium above this is a red flag.

✓ 3. Industry Expertise

The best partners have vertical-specific knowledge — pre-built prompt libraries, custom GPT templates, and deployment patterns tuned to your industry, not generic AI advice.

✓ 4. Free Onboarding & Training

Onboarding, admin setup, team training, and a prompt starter kit should all be included at no additional cost. If they're charging for these, walk away.

✓ 5. Named Human Advisor

You should get a real person — not a ticket queue or chatbot — assigned to your account who learns your business and helps drive adoption over time.

Red Flags to Avoid

  • Charging above $25/$20 per user per month — authorized partners cannot mark up OpenAI pricing
  • Setup fees or onboarding charges — these should be included free
  • Consulting retainer required — standard partner support should come with the subscription
  • No named human advisor — if support goes to a generic ticket queue, you're not getting partner value
  • No industry-specific expertise — generic "AI consulting" without vertical knowledge delivers 40% less ROI in the first 90 days
  • Claims to be an "OpenAI partner" without clarifying which program — ask specifically: "Are you in the OpenAI SMB Channel Partner Program?"

Does the partner earn commission from my subscription?

Yes — OpenAI compensates authorized partners through a revenue-share arrangement for the first 24 months of a customer's subscription. You pay the same price regardless; OpenAI shares a portion of their revenue with the partner as compensation for deployment and support services. This is why partners can afford to provide free onboarding and training.

Can I switch from a partner to OpenAI direct (or vice versa) later?

Yes. ChatGPT Business subscriptions can be managed directly through OpenAI at any time. If you started through a partner and want to manage billing directly, you can do so through your ChatGPT Business admin settings. There are no lock-ins or transfer fees.

What happens after 24 months of the partner arrangement?

After 24 months, the OpenAI revenue-share with the partner typically ends, though this does not affect your subscription price or access. ElevaIQ.com continues to support customers past the revenue-share window because long-term relationships and customer outcomes drive referrals. Your price remains $25/user/month (monthly) or $20/user/month (annual).
