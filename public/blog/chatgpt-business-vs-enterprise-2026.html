ChatGPT now has five different pricing plans, each designed for different users and teams. Choosing the right one can save thousands of dollars or help you scale faster.

This guide breaks down every ChatGPT plan—what you get, what it costs, and who it's for. By the end, you'll know exactly which one fits your needs and budget.

ChatGPT Free: The Starter

ChatGPT Free

$0/month

For testing and learning

What You Get

  • Access to GPT-4o and GPT-4 Turbo (with usage limits)
  • Basic web browsing and file upload
  • Limited conversation history
  • No mobile app priority
  • Shared usage limits

Who It's For

Individual users experimenting with ChatGPT. Students, casual learners, or anyone testing AI before committing to a paid plan.

Real Cost vs Benefit

Free is great for learning, but usage limits can be frustrating. You'll run into message caps during peak hours. Best as a trial before upgrading to Plus.

ChatGPT Plus: The Individual Professional

ChatGPT Plus

$20/month

For individual power users

What You Get

  • Unlimited access to GPT-4o and GPT-4 Turbo
  • Priority access to new features
  • Advanced analysis and file handling
  • Custom GPTs (no sharing)
  • Higher usage limits than Free
  • DALL-E 3 image generation
  • Mobile app access

Who It's For

Solo professionals doing intensive AI work: freelance writers, developers, consultants, researchers. Anyone who uses ChatGPT daily and needs reliability without team features.

Realistic Cost

$20/month = $240/year. Pays for itself if you save 2-3 hours per week on writing, code, or analysis work.

ChatGPT Pro: The Power User

ChatGPT Pro

$200/month

For extremely heavy individual usage

What You Get

  • Everything in Plus
  • 5x higher usage limits
  • Advanced reasoning and analysis
  • Priority compute resources
  • Longest context windows available
  • Priority access to experimental models

Who It's For

Individual researchers, advanced developers, or AI researchers who need the absolute highest usage limits and latest experimental models. This is for edge cases, not standard business use.

Is It Worth $200/Month?

Only if you're a solo AI researcher, developer running 100+ generations per day, or publishing papers using ChatGPT's analysis. For 99% of professionals, Plus is sufficient.

ChatGPT Business: The Team Sweet Spot

ChatGPT Business

$30/user/month

For teams of 2-149 people

What You Get

  • Everything in Plus per team member
  • Admin controls and team management
  • Data privacy: conversations not used for training
  • Shared workspaces for collaboration
  • Custom GPTs shared across team
  • Usage analytics and admin dashboard
  • SSO and compliance integrations
  • Team member invite limit: 149

Who It's For

Small and mid-market teams (2-149 people) doing any mix of customer service, content creation, coding, analysis, or research. The default choice for most organizations.

Why Business Makes Sense

Compared to Plus ($20/user) + business overhead, the extra $10/user/month gets you:

  • Admin controls to manage security and usage
  • Data privacy (no training on your data)
  • Shared workspaces and team resources
  • Custom GPTs for your specific workflows
  • Compliance features for regulated industries

Example Math: A team of 10 costs $300/month ($30 × 10). That's $3,600/year—often less than hiring one contractor for a few weeks.

ChatGPT Enterprise: The Enterprise Grade

ChatGPT Enterprise

Custom pricing (typically $55-100+/user)

For large organizations with strict compliance needs

What You Get

  • Everything in Business
  • 150+ team member capacity
  • Dedicated support team
  • Advanced security and compliance controls
  • SOC 2 Type II, HIPAA, FedRAMP certification
  • Deployment options and custom integrations
  • Usage and policy enforcement dashboards
  • Advanced audit logging
  • SLA guarantees

Who It's For

Large organizations (150+ users) with strict compliance requirements. Common in healthcare (HIPAA), government (FedRAMP), and finance (SOC 2) industries.

When to Upgrade from Business

Business works fine up to 149 users. Only upgrade to Enterprise when:

  • You need 150+ team members
  • You're in healthcare, finance, or government with compliance mandates
  • You need dedicated support (not just standard support)
  • You require custom integrations or deployment options

Decision Matrix: Which Plan Should You Choose?

Quick Decision Guide

Are you a solo user?

Yes → Are you researching or learning?

  • Yes → ChatGPT Free or Plus
  • No, I use it daily for work → ChatGPT Plus ($20/month)
  • No, I'm a power user doing intensive work → ChatGPT Pro ($200/month, rare)
Do you have a team of 2-149 people?

Yes → ChatGPT Business ($30/user/month)

  • You get admin controls, data privacy, shared workspaces, and everything your team needs to collaborate on AI.
Do you have 150+ people or work in regulated industries?

Yes → ChatGPT Enterprise (custom pricing)

  • Healthcare, finance, government, or any organization requiring HIPAA, FedRAMP, or SOC 2 compliance → Enterprise

Why ChatGPT Business Is the Sweet Spot for Small Teams

If you're evaluating ChatGPT for your company, here's why Business is almost always the right choice for 2-149 people:

1. Cost

At $30/user/month, you're paying only $10 more than Plus but getting team collaboration, admin controls, and data privacy. For comparison, Microsoft Copilot for Microsoft 365 costs $50-65/user when you add base subscriptions.

2. Data Privacy

Business enables "No Data Training"—your conversations will never be used to improve OpenAI's models. This is critical for teams handling customer data, trade secrets, or confidential client work. Plus doesn't have this option.

3. Admin Controls

Manage who has access, set usage limits, enable SSO, and monitor spending from a centralized dashboard. Plus users get none of this.

4. Team Collaboration

Shared workspaces let your entire team see conversations, custom GPTs, and resources. Build a library of prompts and workflows that everyone can use.

5. Custom GPTs

Create AI agents for specific tasks (customer support, content drafting, code review) and share them with your entire team. Plus allows custom GPTs but no sharing.

6. Flexibility

No annual contract. Add or remove team members anytime. If you grow to 150+ people, you can upgrade to Enterprise. If you shrink, downgrade without penalties.

Comparison: Business vs The Alternatives

Business vs Plus (for teams)

  • Plus × 10 users = $200/month, but no team features or data privacy
  • Business × 10 users = $300/month, includes everything plus admin controls and collaboration
  • Winner: Business ($100/month extra for all the team infrastructure)

Business vs Microsoft Copilot

  • Business = $30/user/month, works with any tool
  • Copilot = $30/user/month PLUS $20-35/user for Microsoft 365 = $50-65/user total
  • Winner: Business (cheaper, not tied to Microsoft ecosystem)

Business vs Enterprise

  • Use Business if you have fewer than 150 users and don't need enterprise compliance
  • Move to Enterprise when you hit 150+ users or need SOC 2/HIPAA/FedRAMP
  • Winner: Business for 95% of companies (Enterprise only when needed)

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between ChatGPT Business and Plus?
ChatGPT Plus ($20/month) is for individual users and includes GPT-4 access and higher usage limits. ChatGPT Business ($30/user/month) is for teams of 2-149 people and includes everything in Plus plus admin controls, no data training (data privacy), shared workspaces, custom GPTs with team sharing, and collaboration features. Business is designed for teams; Plus is for individuals.
What is ChatGPT Pro and is it worth $200 per month?
ChatGPT Pro ($200/month) is for individual power users who need the absolute latest models and 5x higher usage limits. It's worth it if you're a solo researcher, developer, or content creator doing extremely intensive work (100+ ChatGPT interactions per day). For most teams and businesses, ChatGPT Business ($30/user/month) provides better value. Pro is rare outside of AI research and very heavy individual usage.
When should I choose ChatGPT Enterprise over Business?
Choose Business if your team has 2-149 people and standard security/compliance needs. Choose Enterprise if you have 150+ users, require advanced security features (SOC 2 Type II, HIPAA, FedRAMP), need a dedicated support team, or work in heavily regulated industries like healthcare, finance, or government. Enterprise provides advanced compliance certifications, custom integrations, and higher SLAs that Business doesn't offer.
Can I mix different ChatGPT plans in my organization?
Yes. You can have some team members on ChatGPT Business while others use Plus individually. However, for best collaboration and admin control, using a single team plan like Business is recommended. This ensures everyone has the same features, data privacy protections, and can access shared resources. You can also have Business users collaborate with external partners who use Plus individually.
What does ChatGPT Business include that Plus doesn't?
ChatGPT Business includes: team admin controls to manage user access and security, data privacy with "No Data Training" option (conversations won't be used for training OpenAI models), shared workspaces for team collaboration on conversations and resources, custom GPTs that can be shared with the entire team, usage analytics and admin dashboard to monitor spending, SSO and compliance integrations, and support for up to 149 team members. Plus is individual-only with none of these team features.
