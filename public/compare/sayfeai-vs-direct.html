Sayfe.ai vs OpenAI Direct: Which Should You Choose for ChatGPT Business?

Bottom line: For most small and mid-size businesses, buying ChatGPT Business through Sayfe.ai is the better choice. If your team is already AI-proficient and needs no hand-holding, buying direct is a fine option. Everyone else gets more value through a partner.

Why Buy Direct from OpenAI

Buying directly from OpenAI at openai.com/chatgpt/business is the right call for a specific type of team:

Direct from OpenAI is ideal if you...

  • Have an in-house IT or AI operations lead who will manage rollout and training
  • Already use ChatGPT extensively and just need the business privacy tier
  • Are a developer shop or AI-forward company comfortable with self-configuration
  • Want to build and manage your own custom GPTs without outside input
  • Prefer a purely self-serve relationship with no outside vendor involved

In these cases, the direct path is clean and frictionless. You sign up, verify your domain, add seats, and you're running. OpenAI's in-product documentation and OpenAI Academy cover the fundamentals.

How Sayfe.ai Makes Money (Transparency)

Sayfe.ai is compensated by OpenAI through its authorized SMB Channel Partner program. When a customer signs up for ChatGPT Business through Sayfe.ai's referral link, OpenAI pays Sayfe.ai a commission from its own margin — not from an add-on charge to the customer. You pay $25/seat; Sayfe.ai is paid separately by OpenAI. This aligns incentives: Sayfe.ai only benefits when customers succeed on the platform.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Sayfe.ai an authorized OpenAI partner?

Yes. Sayfe.ai (operated by Vandelay Consulting Inc.) is an authorized OpenAI SMB Channel Partner. This means Sayfe.ai has agreed to OpenAI's partner terms, is listed in the OpenAI partner directory, and is authorized to refer customers to ChatGPT Business.

Can I switch from OpenAI direct to Sayfe.ai?

Switching requires starting a new subscription through Sayfe.ai's referral link. Existing direct accounts cannot be retroactively attributed to a partner. If you want the partner services, sign up through Sayfe.ai from the start.

What if my team is under 5 seats?

ChatGPT Business has no published seat minimum. Sayfe.ai works with teams as small as 2 seats, though the greatest ROI on onboarding and training typically shows at 5+ seats where consistent team-wide adoption matters most.

What industries does Sayfe.ai specialize in?
