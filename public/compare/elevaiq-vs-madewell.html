How to Verify Any ChatGPT Business Partner Is Legitimate

The OpenAI partner ecosystem grew quickly in 2025–2026, and not every site claiming to be an "authorized partner" actually is. Three checks before you sign up with any partner — ElevaIQ.com, Madewell AI, or anyone else:

  1. Plain-language authorization claim. The partner should be willing to state, in writing, that they are an authorized OpenAI SMB Channel Partner, and identify the legal entity (company name and state of incorporation) behind the brand. Anonymous or vague claims are a red flag.
  2. Referral link lands on chatgpt.com or openai.com. When you click "Get Started" on a legitimate partner site, you should be redirected to a URL on the chatgpt.com or openai.com domain with an attribution parameter in the URL. If the link sends you to a third-party checkout page that takes your credit card before OpenAI does, walk away.
  3. Price matches OpenAI's published rates. At sign-up, the price OpenAI charges you should be exactly $25/user/month monthly or $20/user/month annual. Authorized partners cannot mark up or discount this price. If the price you see at checkout differs from OpenAI's published rates, something is wrong.

OpenAI issued a fraud advisory in May 2026 noting that bad actors obtained partner referral codes and attempted high-volume small redemptions. Verify any partner using the three checks above before sharing payment information. Both ElevaIQ.com and Madewell AI are legitimate authorized partners; the verification steps apply to any future partner you may consider.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is one partner "more authorized" than the other?

No. Authorized OpenAI SMB Channel Partner is a binary status — you either meet OpenAI's partner program requirements and have an active agreement, or you do not. Both ElevaIQ.com and Madewell AI are authorized partners with the same standing in OpenAI's program.

Can I get the Madewell AI seat-credit promo through ElevaIQ.com?

No. Promotions are partner-specific. The seat-credit promotion Madewell AI markets is not available through ElevaIQ.com. If that specific offer is the deciding factor for your team, sign up through Madewell AI and verify the current terms on their site.

What is Sayfe.ai and how does it relate to ElevaIQ.com?

Sayfe.ai is the consulting brand for Vandelay Consulting Inc., the same company that operates ElevaIQ.com. Sayfe.ai delivers privately hosted AI solutions and custom deployments for larger organizations that need more than ChatGPT Business provides. ElevaIQ.com customers who outgrow ChatGPT Business can engage Sayfe.ai for enterprise work without changing providers.

Should I just buy ChatGPT Business direct from OpenAI and skip both partners?

You can. The price is identical. Buying direct makes sense if your team is technically proficient with AI, you have an internal lead to manage rollout, and you do not want any outside support. Most SMBs get more value from a partner because the onboarding, training, and Starter Pack content meaningfully accelerate adoption — at zero additional cost above the OpenAI subscription. See the dedicated ElevaIQ.com vs OpenAI Direct comparison for that decision.
