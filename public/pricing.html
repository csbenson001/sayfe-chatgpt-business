Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from buying ChatGPT Business directly from OpenAI? ∨

The ChatGPT Business subscription itself costs exactly the same ($30/user/month) whether you buy from OpenAI directly or through ElevaIQ.ai. The key difference is that ElevaIQ.ai, as an official OpenAI partner, includes expert implementation services at no additional cost: dedicated AI success advisors, custom prompt libraries, team training, and ongoing optimization support. You get the same product plus expert guidance to maximize your ROI.

Are there any hidden fees or annual contracts? ∨

No. Our pricing is completely transparent at $30 per user per month. Billing is monthly, and you can cancel anytime without penalties or long-term commitments. There are no setup fees, implementation fees, or hidden costs. What you see is what you pay.

What's included in the "Dedicated AI Success Advisor" service? ∨

Your dedicated advisor provides quarterly business reviews, identifies new use cases within your organization, helps with team adoption strategies, answers technical questions, and provides ongoing support. They act as your internal champion for AI adoption and ROI maximization.

Can you provide custom prompt libraries for my specific industry? ∨

Yes. We work with your team to develop industry-specific prompt templates and best practices. These are tailored to your workflows, terminology, and compliance requirements. We provide documentation, examples, and training to ensure your team can use them effectively.

What security and compliance features are included? ∨

ChatGPT Business includes data residency controls, user role management, activity logging, and SOC 2 Type II compliance. We help configure these features for your organization and ensure your admin controls are properly set up. Enterprise customers may require additional compliance configurations.

How do I manage team members and permissions? ∨

ChatGPT Business includes admin controls for managing team members, setting usage policies, and controlling feature access. You can add/remove users, set spending limits, manage shared resources, and configure SSO. Our team helps with the initial setup and provides training on these tools.

What's the typical onboarding timeline? ∨

Most organizations are fully onboarded within 2-4 weeks. This includes account setup, SSO integration (if applicable), initial training session, and configuration. We customize the timeline based on your organization's size and complexity.

Can I switch from ChatGPT Plus or upgrade from a free account? ∨

Absolutely. We help migrate users from individual ChatGPT Plus subscriptions to ChatGPT Business team accounts. Your existing conversations and custom GPTs can be transferred. We handle the technical migration and ensure a smooth transition for your team.

How do you handle data privacy and compliance? ∨

ChatGPT Business offers data privacy controls where conversations are not used to train OpenAI models. We help ensure your team understands the privacy controls and we configure admin settings to enforce your data policies. For additional compliance needs, we can discuss Enterprise options.
