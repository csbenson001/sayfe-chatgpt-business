What "ChatGPT for SMB" Actually Means

ChatGPT for small and medium business typically refers to ChatGPT Business — OpenAI's mid-market tier for teams of 2 to 149 users at $25 per user per month (monthly) or $20 per user per month (annual). It is distinct from ChatGPT Plus (individual use) and ChatGPT Enterprise (organizations of 150+).

ChatGPT Business includes admin controls, SAML SSO, shared workspaces, team Custom GPTs, OpenAI Workspace Agents, contractual data privacy (OpenAI does not train models on customer conversations by default), SOC 2 Type 2 compliance, AES-256 encryption at rest, and TLS 1.2+ in transit.

ElevaIQ.com is a Houston-based authorized OpenAI SMB Channel Partner deploying ChatGPT Business to small and mid-size teams across all 50 U.S. states. We charge nothing on top of OpenAI's direct pricing — the value we add is industry-specific onboarding, custom GPTs, training, governance configuration, and a dedicated AI Success Advisor, all included with the subscription.
