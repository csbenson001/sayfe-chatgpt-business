What is ChatGPT Business?
ChatGPT Business is OpenAI's team plan designed for small and medium businesses with up to 149 seats. It provides enterprise-grade AI capabilities at an affordable per-user price. Key features include:
  • GPT-5.5 and all advanced OpenAI models
  • Admin controls for centralized team management
  • Data privacy — your business data and conversations are never used to train OpenAI's models
  • SSO integration for secure authentication
  • Shared custom GPTs and team workspaces
  • Higher usage limits than the individual Plus plan
It is the recommended AI solution for companies that want the power of ChatGPT with business-grade security and administration.
