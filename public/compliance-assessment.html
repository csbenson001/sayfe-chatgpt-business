    AI compliance assessment: common questions

    Is the assessment really free, and do I need to sign up?

    Yes, it is completely free, and you do not need to sign up to see your results. It takes about 5 minutes, runs entirely in your browser, and shows your AI compliance risk score and action plan instantly with no account required. You only enter an email if you want to download the editable AI policy templates and a copy of your full report.

    What regulations does it cover?

    The Colorado AI Act (now SB 26-189, the ADMT act, effective January 1, 2027), the EU AI Act (transparency obligations effective August 2, 2026), NYC Local Law 144 (automated employment decision tools, which applies regardless of company size), the FCC one-to-one consent rule (effective January 27, 2026), federal non-discrimination requirements, and HIPAA for businesses using AI with health data.

    Does my small business actually have to comply?

    Many small businesses are covered without realizing it. NYC Local Law 144 applies to any employer using an automated employment decision tool regardless of size. Colorado's SB 26-189 (ADMT) and the EU AI Act also reach small businesses that use AI to influence consequential decisions or that serve customers in those jurisdictions. The assessment tells you which rules likely apply to your situation.

    What do I get at the end?

    An instant AI compliance risk score (0–100) with a risk tier, a breakdown of which regulations likely apply, your biggest gaps, and a prioritized action plan.
