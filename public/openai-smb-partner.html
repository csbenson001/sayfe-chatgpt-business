Definition

What an Authorized OpenAI SMB Channel Partner Actually Means

An authorized OpenAI SMB Channel Partner is a firm formally recognized by OpenAI to help small and medium businesses (1–149 seats — the size band that fits the ChatGPT Business tier) deploy, configure, and adopt ChatGPT Business. The program sits alongside OpenAI's enterprise channel (which includes resellers like PwC for ChatGPT Enterprise) but is targeted at the SMB segment.

Authorized partners are bound by OpenAI's SMB Channel Partner Marketing Guidelines, including a strict pricing parity rule (no markup over OpenAI direct pricing — partners deliver services value, not reseller margin) and standards for how the partnership status can be claimed and represented in marketing.

Sayfe.ai is an authorized OpenAI SMB Channel Partner and part of the Sayfe.ai family. Our SMB practice was founded to translate the AI agent expertise Sayfe.ai delivers to SAP and Salesforce enterprise customers into a service tier that fits small and mid-size teams — at no premium over what those teams would pay OpenAI directly. Buyer's Checklist

5 Questions to Ask Any OpenAI Partner Before You Sign

Not all OpenAI partners are created equal. Use these to filter before you commit.

1
Is the partner formally authorized by OpenAI?
Many firms list "OpenAI services" without a partnership. Ask for partner-program confirmation. Sayfe.ai: yes, authorized.
2
Do they charge a markup over OpenAI direct pricing?
OpenAI's partner program requires price parity. Any partner offering "BOGO" or discount licenses is operating outside the SMB partner guidelines. Sayfe.ai: zero markup — same $25/$20 as direct.
3
Do they include onboarding, training, and starter content free?
Partners that bill for setup hourly are just a consultancy. The model is supposed to bundle services value into the subscription. Sayfe.ai: AI Success Advisor, industry starter pack, training all included at no extra cost.
4
Do they have industry depth or just generic offerings?
Generic AI services do not address the workflows that matter in your vertical (HIPAA in healthcare, BAA workflows, ABA rules in legal, RFI patterns in construction, etc.). Sayfe.ai: 16 dedicated industry pages, vertical-specific prompt libraries, custom GPTs for each — including deep Houston energy specialty.
5
Are they SOC 2 / compliance-aware in how they configure deployments?
Default ChatGPT Business is enterprise-grade (SOC 2 Type 2, AES-256, TLS 1.2+, no model training on customer data), but admin controls and SAML SSO must be configured correctly. Sayfe.ai: configures these for you during onboarding; for HIPAA workflows, we set up parallel ChatGPT for Healthcare deployment with BAA.
