Will ChatGPT replace human creative work at a marketing agency?
No — the consensus across the 2026 marketing-AI literature (M1-Project, WebFX, ChatGPT Marketer, Lilach Bullock, Selzy) is the same: use ChatGPT for repeatable, time-heavy work (first drafts, repurposing, research summaries, audience and idea generation) while keeping strategy, judgment, and final edits with humans.
