The Strategy in 60 Seconds

Channel priority: Google Search Ads first (capture intent), ChatGPT Ads second (reach 400M+ free-tier users during active AI research), LinkedIn third (vertical targeting), retargeting on Meta fourth (cheap reinforcement). Skip broad social awareness spend entirely — the unit economics don't justify it for a $30/seat referral product.

🆕 ChatGPT Ads: OpenAI launched a self-serve Ads Manager at ads.openai.com — no minimum spend, CPC bidding ($3-5 range). Ads show to Free and Go tier users only — exactly the audience that hasn't upgraded to Business yet. Contextual targeting means your ad appears when someone is literally asking ChatGPT about business tools, team productivity, or AI adoption.

The math that makes this work: One converted customer with 10 seats generates $150/month in commission for Year 1, then $60/month for Year 2. That's $2,520 lifetime commission per customer. If you acquire that customer for $50-150 in ad spend, you're looking at a 15-45x return. Even a single conversion per month at $100 CAC makes this campaign profitable from day one.

Why Google Search wins: People actively searching "chatgpt for teams" or "chatgpt business pricing" have purchase intent. The average Google Search CPC is $2-6 for these keywords. And these searchers are already educated on the product; they just need a trusted partner to guide them through.

Critical dependency: This entire strategy activates the moment you get OpenAI partner approval. The approval badge ("Official OpenAI Partner") is your #1 conversion asset — it's the trust signal that separates you from generic AI consulting sites. Without it, the landing pages still work, but conversion rates will be significantly lower.

Why ChatGPT Ads Are Perfect for ElevaIQ

The Strategic Advantage

Audience alignment is near-perfect: ChatGPT ads only show to Free and Go tier users. These are people who already use ChatGPT personally but haven't upgraded to a paid business plan. They're your exact target — they already know the product works, they just need to be told about the Business tier and guided through the upgrade.

Contextual targeting = intent matching: Unlike Google where you bid on keywords, ChatGPT matches your ad to the conversation topic. When someone asks ChatGPT "how do I get my team using AI?" or "what's the best AI tool for my business?", your ElevaIQ ad appears right below the response. This is closer to "intercepting a buying conversation" than any other ad platform.

First-mover advantage: Most small businesses haven't discovered this platform yet. The early advertiser pool is small, which means lower competition and potentially lower CPCs than they'll be in 12 months.

Key limitation: You cannot target specific demographics, job titles, or industries on ChatGPT Ads. Targeting is purely contextual — based on what the user is asking about in that moment. This means your ad copy must be broadly appealing while your landing page does the industry-specific conversion work.
